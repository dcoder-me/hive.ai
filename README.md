Splitting money with the people you live and travel with shouldn't mean spreadsheets, nagging, or learning a "finance app." With Hive, you just **chat** — and an AI quietly turns your messages into expenses, balances, and a running tally of who owes what.

Built for **small, trusting groups of 3–5** — flatmates, families, friends on a trip — and simple enough that everyone from a CS student to their grandfather can use it without help.

## How it works

```
"paid 1200 for dinner"  →  🤖 reads it  →  💸 splits it  →  ✅ all square
```

1. **Chat** like you always do — type what you spent, in plain language.
2. **Track** — Hive understands the amount, the split, and who paid.
3. **Settle** — see who owes what, mark it paid, done.

> No forms. No receipts to scan. No payment gateway. Just text in, clarity out.
